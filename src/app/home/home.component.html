It's easy!
First, you need a small object (like a coin), and at least two people (e.g. you and a friend);
Second, the person who is going to hunt the treasure needs to wait in a small room (e.g. bathroom);
Third, the other person will hide the chosen object in the house

Fifth, if you like the picture, choose one of the filters and the time the treasure hunter has to look for the object
Sixth, press start and give the phone to your friend, he/she has to find the giving object using the picture as a clue, the filter will slowly fade out.
Your friend should press "found it!" when he/she finds the object!
